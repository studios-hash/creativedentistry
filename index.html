Frequently Asked Questions
How early do I need to arrive to fill out my new patient intake forms?

We strictly protect your schedule and keep wait times minimal. Our office utilizes a paperless, fully digital check-in; we text secure data links directly to your personal mobile device, allowing you to complete your info and medical history securely from home before your appointment. We also have iPads in the office if you do not have your phone with you to fill out these forms.

What is your office policy regarding appointment cancellations or modifications?

Because we dedicate a substantial block of unhurried time exclusively to you and never double-book our providers, we strictly require a 48 hour notice to cancel or reschedule a reservation to avoid a $125.00 fee. You have total flexibility to coordinate adjustments with our desk via text, email, or a standard phone call.

Which public transportation lines run closest to your building entrance?

Our building sits steps from the Borough Hall Station (servicing the 2, 3, 4, and 5 trains) and the Court Street Station (servicing the M and R lines). We are also a brief 3-minute walk from the Jay Street–Borough Hall Station (A, C, and F lines) and are directly served by local surface bus routes B25, B26, B38, B41, B51, and B52.

Is there a safe place to park my car or secure a bicycle outside your office?

Yes. A secure, fully staffed paid indoor valet parking garage is located directly across the street from our front entrance on Montague Street, allowing for seamless driving arrival. Additionally, an active NYC CitiBike docking station sits directly outside our main building entrance doors.

How does your multi-doctor team handle dental emergencies?

Emergency dental care is given strict priority on our schedule. We reserve dedicated blocks every single day to ensure that patients experiencing severe toothaches, infection swelling, or broken teeth can secure same-day stabilization, advanced pain relief, and immediate diagnostic clarity. Should you have a dental emergency, always call us at (718) 875-9424, even if it is past our office hours.
