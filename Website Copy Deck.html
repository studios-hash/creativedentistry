Overview

Overview

Heading

Prevention is the most powerful dentistry there is

Lead paragraph

Most of what threatens your smile — decay, gum disease, a small crack — is far easier and far gentler to address when we catch it early. That is the entire point of a regular visit here.

Body paragraph

Every six months we take a complete look: a thorough exam, a professional cleaning, digital X-rays only when they are warranted, and an honest conversation about anything we see. No upsell, no scare tactics — just a clear picture of where you stand.

What's Included
  • Comprehensive exams — a full evaluation of teeth, gums, bite, and soft tissue.
  • Professional cleanings — gentle, thorough hygiene that removes what brushing cannot.
  • Digital X-rays — low-radiation imaging, taken only when clinically useful.
  • Oral-cancer screening — a quick, painless check at every exam.
  • Gum health — periodontal evaluation to keep your foundation strong.
  • Sealants & fluoride — simple protection for vulnerable teeth.

Eyebrow

Why It Matters

Heading

Care that prevents, not just repairs

Body

A steady rhythm of preventive visits is the single best investment you can make in your smile — it keeps small things small, and keeps you out of the chair for the big ones.

FAQ

How often should I come in?

For most patients, every six months. If you are managing gum disease or other concerns, we may suggest a tailored interval.

Do you take X-rays every visit?

No. We take digital X-rays only when there is a clinical reason — and they are low-radiation when we do.

I have not been to a dentist in years. Is that a problem?

Not at all, and you will not hear a word of judgment. We simply start with a complete picture and build a plan from there.

The closest thing to your natural tooth

Lead paragraph

An implant replaces the whole tooth — root and crown — so you can eat, speak, and smile without a second thought. It is the closest thing modern dentistry has to giving you your natural tooth back.

Body paragraph

Dr. Bellafiore plans every case in three dimensions with CBCT imaging and surgical guides, so placement is precise and predictable. The result is a restoration built to last for decades.

What's Included
  • Single-tooth implants — replace one tooth without touching the healthy ones beside it.
  • Implant-supported bridges — replace several teeth with a stable, fixed solution.
  • Full-arch restoration — a fixed set of teeth on a few well-placed implants.
  • CBCT-guided planning — 3D imaging and surgical guides for precise placement.
  • Bone grafting — rebuilding support where it is needed for a strong base.
  • Implant maintenance — ongoing care to keep your implants healthy for life.

FAQ

Is the Botox® treatment safe?

Yes, when administered by a trained clinician. We use it precisely to relax specific jaw muscles, with no effect on your expression.

Will a nightguard really help?

For most people who clench or grind, yes — it protects your teeth and often relieves morning pain and headaches.

Could my headaches be dental?

They can be. Clenching and a strained bite are common, under-recognized causes. An evaluation will tell us.
