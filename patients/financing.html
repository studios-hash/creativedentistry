Financial Flexibility

Affordable Dental Financing for Every Budget

Cost should never function as a barrier to the high-quality dental care you deserve. We partner with multiple trusted, healthcare-specific financing platforms to break your treatments down into manageable, budget-friendly monthly payments.

Contact Our Office

Straightforward Treatment Estimates: No Pricing Fluff

At Creative Dentistry of Brooklyn Heights, our standard of care is driven by strict operational honesty. Before any clinical treatment begins, our doctors provide you with a clear, itemized list of necessary steps and precise costs. We do not engage in hidden administrative fees or over-diagnosis. Our billing coordinators sit with you to map out your estimated out-of-pocket tracking, ensuring you remain entirely informed and in control of your finances.

Seamless Monthly Payments via Cherry & Sunbit Financing

For our patients undertaking comprehensive cosmetic smile makeovers, advanced clear aligner therapies, surgical dental implants, or complex full-mouth reconstructions, we offer fast integration with Cherry and Sunbit financing. Our front desk team can instantly assist you in reviewing your options securely using our in-office iPads. These modern platforms feature exceptionally high approval rates, instant credit decisions, and allow soft credit checks that protect your credit score during application.

Financial Accessibility Reimagined

We accept multiple modern, secure payment modalities across our practice, ensuring that your billing experience is as smooth and comfortable as our windowed operatory suites. Our payment tracks are custom-tailored for:

  • Patients undergoing necessary restorative or surgical treatments who want to split costs into comfortable monthly payments.
  • Insured patients looking to fund their remaining out-of-pocket estimated PPO co-pays or deductibles smoothly.
  • Tech-conscious neighbors who prefer utilizing swift contactless payment configurations, including Apple Pay and secure mobile checkouts.
  • Uninsured community members looking to combine a monthly payment setup with upfront preventive wellness discounts (Explore our private In-House Membership Plan).

Secure Instant Financing Approval Today

Don't delay necessary dental care until it evolves into a painful, complex emergency. Speak with our gracious front desk team today or use our in-office iPads to structure an easy payment plan through Cherry or Sunbit in minutes.
