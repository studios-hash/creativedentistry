Processing Out-of-Network PPO Claims Seamlessly

Our practice operates successfully with the vast majority of major commercial PPO dental insurance plans every day. Because we are an independent, out-of-network provider for select corporate carriers, Dr. Bellafiore, Dr. Black, and Dr. Baer can choose the right materials and treatments, without insurance-driven shortcuts. That freedom lets us focus entirely on delivering exceptional dental care and craftsmanship for you. As a complimentary courtesy, our billing staff manages the complete back-and-forth claim submission process, first confirming your available PPO out-of-network benefits in advance so you clearly understand exactly what your plan includes.

Proud Direct Network Providers for Delta Dental & CSEA

To provide maximum localized value to our Brooklyn civil servants, union members, and families, we maintain direct, in-network participation with Delta Dental and CSEA. If you are covered under these specific networks, our coordinators will optimize your direct coverage smoothly, ensuring your preventive cleanings, diagnostic digital records, and subsequent treatments align perfectly with your allowed plan benefits.

Secure Online Insurance Verification

Want to verify your dental benefits before stepping foot in our office? Complete our secure, digital insurance verification form below. Once submitted, our expert billing coordinators will directly review your plan's allowances, handle the administrative cross-checking, and prepare a transparent, upfront breakdown of your estimated coverage before your comprehensive visit begins.

Your privacy is our utmost priority. All information submitted through this portal is fully encrypted, secure, and managed in strict compliance with federal healthcare privacy standards.

Stress-Free Insurance Optimization

We believe that dental coverage should serve as a useful tool that supports your preventive health, never an obstacle that delays necessary care. Our billing infrastructure is great for:

  • Patients with corporate PPO insurance who want a private office that executes complimentary claims filing and coordinates paperwork directly.
  • Brooklyn civic servants, municipal workers, and families looking to utilize direct in-network benefits with Delta Dental or CSEA.
  • Individuals who demand a clear, upfront estimate of costs and expected reimbursements before any clinical treatment procedure begins.
  • Neighbors without traditional insurance who want validation that a lack of commercial coverage will not block them from accessing high-tier dentistry (See our private In-House Membership Plan).
