Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your administrative policy if I need to cancel or modify a scheduled visit?

Because we dedicate generous blocks of unhurried, one-on-one time exclusively to you and strictly avoid double-booking our doctors, we maintain a firm 48 hour notice policy for all appointment adjustments. Canceling or rescheduling a block with less than 48 hours' notice will incur a transparent $125.00 fee. You can easily modify your slot with our front desk via text message, email, or a phone call.

Can I complete all of my required medical history paperwork before arriving at the office?

Yes, we actively encourage it to protect your time. Our intake platform is entirely digital and paper-free. Prior to your appointment window, our coordinators text a secure link straight to your personal mobile device, letting you complete your records seamlessly from home.

How do I coordinate emergency dental care if I experience a crisis outside of hours?

If you experience a sudden toothache, infection, or dental trauma outside of regular hours, please call our main line at (718) 875-9424 and leave a detailed message. Our team actively monitors urgent notifications and will connect with you to coordinate a same-day stabilization slot. If you face a life-threatening medical emergency or severe physical jaw trauma, dial 911 or visit a hospital emergency room immediately.

Do you have dedicated parking spaces built directly into your building?

While our office building does not feature a private parking lot, a fully staffed, secure paid indoor valet parking garage is located directly across the street from our front entrance on Montague Street. This allows for seamless driving arrival without the hassle of street-parking searches.

What languages can your communication and clinical staff seamlessly speak?

To ensure that every single Brooklyn neighbor feels completely respected, heard, and accurately understood without barriers, our front office and clinical support staff can fully communicate and explain your care parameters in English, Spanish, and Polish.
