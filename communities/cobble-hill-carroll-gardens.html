Seamless Local Access from Cobble Hill & Carroll Gardens

Traveling from the vibrant, tree-lined brownstone blocks of Cobble Hill or Carroll Gardens to our office is exceptionally straightforward, as our physical suite sits just north of your neighborhood boundary at 185 Montague Street, 3rd Floor. If you are commuting via public transit, simply hop on the uptown F or G train at Carroll Street or Bergen Street and ride it directly to the Jay Street–Borough Hall hub. From there, it is a brief, flat two-block walk past local shops to our entrance next to Chipotle. If you are pushing a stroller or walking, follow the direct North-South corridors of either Court Street or Smith Street straight up toward Borough Hall, crossing over Atlantic Avenue. For patients driving, the commute takes less than 5 minutes, and you can bypass street parking entirely by pulling directly into the secure valet garage directly opposite our front doors.

Dental Solutions Engineered for Cobble Hill & Carroll Gardens Lifestyles

Our patient community from the Cobble Hill and Carroll Gardens sectors often centers around active family households, multi-generational families, and busy local business owners who demand long-term clinical honesty over corporate sales tactics. We prioritize conservative, highly durable treatment pathways that respect your family's baseline values:

  • Comprehensive Multi-Generational Family Care — From conservative pediatric wellness cleanings to advanced root canal therapy and restorative implants, our doctors manage your entire household's oral ecosystem under one stable roof.
  • Hospital-Honed Prosthodontics & Dentures — Restore completely stable speech, full chewing mechanics, and natural aesthetics. Dr. Baer utilizes her specialized Chief Residency prosthodontic training to craft meticulously fitted partials, full complete dentures, and secure implant-supported overdentures.
  • Minimally Invasive Laser Periodontics — Protect your underlying bone matrix comfortably. We have swapped out traditional, painful gum surgery methods for the advanced Biolase Waterlase Laser, clearing away deep sub-gingival infections with minimal swelling and a fast, comfortable recovery.

Transparent Pricing

We back our multi-doctor clinical care with clear, straightforward dental economics.
