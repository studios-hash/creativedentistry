As a long-standing fixture of the neighborhood, our office is integrated directly into the historic heart of the community at 185 Montague Street, 3rd Floor, right adjacent to Chipotle. Whether you are walking over from the brownstones on Pierrepont, Hicks, or Henry Street, or heading up from a stroll along the Brooklyn Heights Promenade, reaching our front doors requires no major commute logistics. Dental Solutions Engineered for Brooklyn Heights Lifestyles

Living in our historic neighborhood means balancing active family dynamics, demanding executive routines, and an appreciation for premium, personalized service. We have curated our clinical menu to address the specific wellness goals of our local families and professionals:

  • Streamlined Family Block Scheduling — We protect your household schedule by offering back-to-back or simultaneous routine cleaning slots for parents and children, managing your family's oral health in a single, highly efficient window.
  • Elite Cosmetic Artistry & Restorations — Correct spacing gaps, asymmetrical smile lines, or internal tooth discoloration with custom, hand-shaded porcelain veneers and lifelike composite bonding designed to look completely natural.
  • Advanced TMJ & Clenching Relief — Combat the physical symptoms of high-stress routines. We combine precise Dawson Academy bite adjustments with specialized hard-acrylic nightguards and therapeutic Allergan Botox® to permanently relieve jaw pain and morning migraines.

Transparent Pricing

Premium family dentistry requires complete financial integrity with absolutely zero billing fluff. For our insured neighbors, we work daily as an out-of-network concierge specialist with major commercial PPOs, managing all complex claims forms on your behalf to maximize your direct reimbursement. We also maintain proud, active in-network standing with Delta Dental and CSEA. If you lack traditional insurance, our private In-House Membership Plan provides a direct safety net for $575 a year, fully covering your routine exams, cleanings, and emergencies while unlocking a flat 15% savings across our entire operational treatment menu. Flexible monthly payment plans are also instantly accessible on our digital tablets through Cherry and Sunbit.
