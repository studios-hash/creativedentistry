Seamless Local Access from DUMBO

While DUMBO is highly celebrated for its historic cobblestone streets, tech sectors, and waterfront parks, finding a private, family-owned dental office within the industrial-conversion blocks can be difficult. Our modern treatment suites sit just up the hill at 185 Montague Street, 3rd Floor, making your commute under 10 minutes. If you are traveling via subway, step into the York Street Station and take the downtown F train exactly one stop to Jay Street–Borough Hall, leaving you just a two-block walk from our building entryway. If you prefer to walk or cycle, head straight south up Washington Street or Adams Street, cross over the BQE underpass, and follow Cadman Plaza directly to Montague Street. A CitiBike docking station is located directly outside our glass lobby doors for a quick, friction-free return ride.

Dental Solutions Engineered for DUMBO Lifestyles

Our patient family from the DUMBO waterfront includes tech innovators, creative executives, and fast-paced professionals who value efficiency, pristine aesthetics, and advanced technology. We prioritize treatments designed to match your specific lifestyle needs:

  • Therapeutic Masseter Botox® for Stress Clenching — High-velocity professional environments frequently lead to unconscious nighttime teeth grinding (bruxism). We utilize targeted Botox® muscle relaxation and custom hard-acrylic bite splints to eliminate severe morning jaw tension, localized stiffness, and stress migraines.
  • Streamlined Family Block Scheduling — We protect your household schedule by offering back-to-back or simultaneous routine cleaning slots for parents and children, managing your family's oral health in a single, highly efficient window.
  • Elite Cosmetic Artistry & Restorations — Correct spacing gaps, asymmetrical smile lines, or internal tooth discoloration with custom, hand-shaded porcelain veneers and lifelike composite bonding designed to look completely natural.
  • Advanced TMJ & Clenching Relief — Combat the physical symptoms of high-stress routines. We combine precise Dawson Academy bite adjustments with specialized hard-acrylic nightguards and therapeutic Allergan Botox® to permanently relieve jaw pain and morning migraines.
