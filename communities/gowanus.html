Seamless Local Access from Gowanus

For residents, local artists, and makers navigating the industrial-chic corridors and new residential spaces of Gowanus, our physical practice offers an unhurried, healthcare sanctuary located just a quick trip away at 185 Montague Street, 3rd Floor. If you are commuting via public transit, head over to the Union Street Station on 4th Avenue and take the uptown R train directly to the Court Street Station. Step off the train, walk less than one block to our building entrance next to Chipotle, and take the passenger elevator straight up to our 3rd-floor suite. If you are driving up from near the canal blocks or the Third Avenue corridor, follow Fourth Avenue or Smith Street straight north toward Borough Hall, the drive takes under 8 minutes, and you can pull directly into the secure paid valet parking garage located directly across the street from our front doors.

Dental Solutions Engineered for Gowanus Lifestyles

The Gowanus community is characterized by independent creators, tech professionals, and new residents who prioritize exceptional value, advanced health diagnostics, and clear financial transparency. We have custom-tailored our private practice menu to perfectly match your lifestyle boundaries:

  • The Private In-House Membership Safety Net — For our neighborhood's freelancers, independent makers, and contractors who lack traditional corporate PPO insurance, our private plan covers your entire baseline year of routine cleanings, comprehensive exams, and emergency x-rays for a flat $575, with zero corporate deductibles or waiting windows.
  • Artistic Cosmetic Resin Bonding & Contouring — Correct minor enamel chips, uneven tooth edges, or small structural gaps in a single, highly conservative appointment using our advanced, tooth-colored composite resins that blend seamlessly with your natural smile anatomy.
  • Advanced 3D CBCT Implant Planning — For missing teeth or failing dental work, we utilize state-of-the-art 3D CBCT imaging to map your precise bone density and facial structures. This allows Dr. Bellafiore to pre-engineer highly predictable, computer-guided titanium implant restorations that feel entirely organic.

Private Practice Accountability Since 1961

Spending a few minutes to commute outside your immediate neighborhood boundary connects you with a rare foundation of private dental stability. While corporate dental chains continue to open across developing industrial zones, our independent private practice has anchored this community for 65 years. Dr. Matthew Bellafiore brings more than three decades of advanced surgical dental implant and complex restorative training to our practice, holding elite credentials with the International Congress of Oral Implantologists (ICOI). He operates side-by-side with Dr. Alexander Black, an expert in computer-guided bite design, and Dr. Becca Baer, a former hospital Chief Resident specifically trained in complex full-mouth prosthodontic rehabilitation, giving you access to an unhurried, multi-doctor triage that treats you like a neighbor, never a generic chart number.

Transparent Pricing

We believe premium, conservative dentistry must always be accompanied by straightforward, honest pricing. If you hold traditional commercial insurance, our front office acts as your administrative liaison, handling all out-of-network PPO filings and paperwork on your behalf to maximize your direct insurance reimbursement. We also maintain proud, active in-network standing with Delta Dental and CSEA. For our uninsured neighbors, our private In-House Membership Plan provides complete preventive peace of mind for an affordable $575 a year, while automatically unlocking a flat 15% discount on all subsequent treatments. Flexible monthly financing with soft credit checks and instant approvals is also fully integrated right on our office tablets through Cherry and Sunbit.
