<h3>General

<p>At
<strong>Creative
Dentistry
of
Brooklyn
Heights</strong>,
we
care
about
all
of
our
patients
and
are
committed
to
facilitating

the
accessibility
and

of
our
website,
<strong>creativedentistrybrooklyn.com</strong>
(the
"
Website
"
).
We
believe
all
of
our
visitors
should
be
able
to
easily
research
our
dental
services,
book
appointments,
and
access
our
digital

our
digital
offerings
are
accessible
to
persons
with
disabilities
including,
but
not
limited
to,
users
of
screen
reader
technology.</p>
 
<h3>Continuous
&
Ongoing
Optimizations</h3>
<p>Please
be
aware
that
our
efforts
to
maintain,
monitor,
and
optimize
web
accessibility
are
ongoing.
Digital
content
is
constantly
evolving,
and
our
team
is
dedicated
to
consistently
updating
the
Website
to
meet
higher
standards.
While
we
strive
to
make
the
Website
as

as

some

as
the
range

is
wide,

and
constantly
changing.
We
appreciate
your
understanding
as
we
work
continuously
to
improve
your
digital
experience.</p>
 
<h3>Conformance
Standards</h3>
<p>To

our
accessibility
goals,
we
strive
to
design
and
maintain
our
Website
in
conformance
with
the
Web
Content
Accessibility
Guidelines
(WCAG)
2
.
2
AA
Level,
which
is
recognized
as
the
international
standard
measure
of
success.
Aligning
with
these
guidelines
helps
bring
our
Website
into
conformance
with
the
Americans
with
Disabilities
Act
(ADA)
of
1990
and
other
relevant
accessibility
regulations.</p>
 
<h3>Accessibility
Features
on
Our
Site</h3>
 
<p>We
use
automated
accessibility
testing
tools
as
well
as
manual
reviews
to
identify
areas
that
need
improvement.
Here
are
some
of
the
features
we
actively
address
to
ensure
our
site
is
accessible
to
all:</p>
<ul>
 
<li><strong>Improved
Site
Structure:</strong>
We
utilize
appropriate
titles,
labels,
and
heading
structures
to
help
users
navigate
our
pages
smoothly
with
assistive
technology.</li>
 
<li><strong>Keyboard
Access:</strong>
The
content
we
build
is
intended
to
be
fully
navigable
and
operational
using
only
a
keyboard.</li>
 
<li><strong>Text
Equivalents:</strong>
Alternative
text
(Alt
text)
is
applied
to
appropriate
images
and
non-text
visual
elements
to
convey
equivalent
information
to
screen
readers.</li>
 
<li><strong>Site
Consistency:</strong>
We
use
repeated
navigational
mechanisms
to
achieve
a
predictable
and
consistent
user
experience
across
the
entire
site.</li>
</ul>
 
<h3>Using
the
Accessibility
Widget</h3>
<p>To
further
enhance
your
experience,
this
Website
utilizes
the
<strong>UserWay
Free
Web
Accessibility
Widget</strong>.
This
software
allows
our
site
to
improve
its
compliance
with
the
Web
Content
Accessibility
Guidelines
(WCAG
2
.
2
).</p>
<p><strong>How
to
open
the
widget:</strong>
The
accessibility
menu
can
be
enabled
by
clicking
the
accessibility
menu
icon
that
appears
on
the
corner
of
the
page.
After
triggering
the
menu,
please
wait
a
moment
for
the
accessibility
menu
to
load
in
its
entirety.
From
there,
you
can
customize
your
experience
by
adjusting
contrast,
text
size,
spacing,
keyboard
navigation,
and
more
to
best
fit
your
personal
needs.</p>
