Urgent Dental Conditions We Treat

Severe Toothaches & Abscess Relief

Persistent, throbbing tooth pain or sudden gum swelling are clear signals from your body that an internal infection is active. We provide rapid diagnostic evaluations to identify the source of infection and administer immediate relief.

Broken, Chipped, or Cracked Teeth

A structurally compromised tooth left untreated can quickly expose sensitive inner nerves to bacteria. We offer durable, fast restorative solutions, including composite fillings and custom dental crowns, to protect your enamel.

Lost Fillings or Loose Crowns

When an old dental restoration fails or falls out, the underlying tooth tissue becomes highly sensitive and vulnerable. We can comfortably recement loose crowns or place durable new restorations the same day.

Routine Root Canal Therapy

If deep tooth decay or internal trauma reaches the inner nerve pulp, we perform gentle, conservative endodontic treatments to remove the damaged tissue and completely eliminate intense throbbing.

Simple Tooth Extractions

In cases where a tooth is severely fractured or non-restorable due to deep decay, our doctors perform gentle, conservative extractions to protect your surrounding bone and oral ecosystem.

Recognizing a True Dental Emergency

Dental emergencies rarely occur at a convenient time, and delaying care can allow a localized infection to spread, resulting in more complex and costly health updates down the road. We maintain an open, judgment-free environment designed to address your pain immediately. If you are experiencing any of the following symptoms, your condition qualifies for an immediate, same-day clinical intervention:

  • Severe, persistent throbbing dental pain that keeps you awake at night or fails to respond to over-the-counter pain relievers.
  • Visible swelling in your face, cheeks, jawline, or gums, which frequently indicates an advancing dental abscess.
  • A tooth that has been broken, fractured, split, or structurally loosened due to sudden physical trauma or a biting accident.
  • The sudden failure or loss of a filling, crown, or fixed bridge that leaves your underlying dentin exposed and highly sensitive.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I experience a dental emergency outside of your regular business hours?

Our Brooklyn Heights office is fully staffed Monday through Thursday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Fridays from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. If your emergency occurs outside of these operating hours or during a major annual holiday, please call our main line at (718) 875-9424 and leave a detailed voicemail. A member of our team actively monitors urgent messages and will get back to you shortly to coordinate your same-day care. Important: If you are experiencing a severe, life-threatening medical emergency, uncontrollable bleeding, or severe facial jaw trauma, please dial 911 or visit the nearest hospital emergency room immediately.

How quickly can I expect to be seen for an urgent emergency appointment?

We reserve dedicated time blocks in our daily schedule specifically to accommodate urgent, same-day dental emergencies. Our primary objective is to fast-track your entry, provide immediate diagnostic clarity, eliminate active pain, and stabilize your oral health on the very same day you contact us.

Do I have to fill out extensive, complicated paperwork while I am in severe dental pain?

No, we have entirely removed administrative friction from our check-in process. Prior to stepping through our doors, we text a secure link directly to your personal mobile device so you can complete your patient info and medical history digitally from home. We also have iPads available in our reception lounge if needed.

Will my out-of-network PPO dental insurance help cover a same-day tooth extraction or root canal?

Yes, the vast majority of commercial PPO insurance plans provide explicit coverage for urgent dental exams, tooth extractions, and root canal procedures. Our expert front desk team will pre-verify your benefits and handle all the claims filing and administrative back-and-forth directly for you to minimize your stress.

How do I find your physical building entrance if I am arriving in a rush?

We are located on the 3rd Floor of 185 Montague Street, in a multi-story office building right next to Chipotle. Take the elevator straight up to the third floor and ring our office doorbell. We sit right outside a CitiBike station, have a paid valet parking lot directly across the street, and are steps from the Borough Hall Station (2, 3, 4, 5 trains) and Court Street (M, R trains).
