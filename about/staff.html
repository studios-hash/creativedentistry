Seamless Financial Coordination

Our front desk staff is highly trained in maximizing your available dental benefits and organizing your billing with no fluff. We handle all out-of-network claims submission paperwork directly on your behalf to minimize your stress (Learn more on our Insurance Page). We also accept Delta Dental and CSEA plans. For uninsured neighbors, our team can instantly walk you through our custom In-House Membership Plan or assist you in applying for easy monthly financing through Cherry and Sunbit with our office iPads (Apply via our Financing Page).
