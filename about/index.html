The true clinical advantage of our practice lies in our unique collaborative model. Rather than evaluating your oral health through a single lens, Dr. Bellafiore, Dr. Black, and Dr. Baer merge their advanced institutional training to cross-examine complex cases as a cohesive clinical triage. This interdisciplinary synergy allows us to fulfill our practice standard: treating every patient with the exact same level of skill, deep compassion, and precision we would provide to our own family members.

A Neighborhood Anchor Since 1961

Creative Dentistry of Brooklyn Heights is not a brand-new, corporate dental clinic managed by a distant entity. Our physical practice footprint holds deep local history, serving our immediate neighbors continuously since 1961. Dr. Matthew Bellafiore has proudly maintained this legacy of trusted private dental care for over 30 years, giving our patients an unmatched level of clinical stability and personal accountability.

The Benchmark for Premium Brooklyn Care

Choosing a dental home is about finding a clinical team that aligns perfectly with your lifestyle, your family's healthcare values, and your long-term wellness goals. At CDBH, we have engineered our multi-doctor practice model to deliver a premium, boutique experience that replaces the clinical rush of corporate dental clinics with relationship-driven care. Our practice is a great fit for:

  • Neighbors seeking an authentic, established private practice rooted deeply in the local Brooklyn Heights community.
  • Families who want a multi-doctor dental home capable of handling routine family cleanings, complex oral surgeries, and cosmetic smile makeovers in one location.
  • Patients looking for a multi-lingual dental staff that communicates clinical options in English, Spanish, and Polish.
  • Individuals requiring flexible, transparent options for processing dental insurance or establishing budget-friendly financing setups.
