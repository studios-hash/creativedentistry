From your very first contact, our team streamlines your administrative logistics. We also provide iPads in our waiting room for those unable to fill their online forms beforehand.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I read independent, verified third-party reviews for your practice online?

We encourage complete transparency. You can read hundreds of unfiltered testimonials from our actual patients directly on our official Google Maps listing, Yelp business profile, or verified healthcare scheduling networks like ZocDoc.

What is your office policy if I need to cancel or adjust a scheduled appointment?

Because we dedicate substantial blocks of unhurried, one-on-one time exclusively to you and never double-book our doctors, we require a strict 48 hour notice to cancel or reschedule to avoid a $125.00 fee. You have total communication flexibility to adjust your visit with our front desk via text, email, or a standard phone call.

How do your reviews reflect your approach to handling severe dental anxiety?

If you explore our patient testimonials, you will see a consistent theme: genuine comfort. We specialize in transforming terrifying dental visits into peaceful experiences by curating a calm environment, using advanced digital tools, offering comforting nitrous oxide, and allowing you to completely set the pace of your own care.

Is your office building fully accessible for patients with mobility limitations?

Yes, absolutely. Our reviews frequently highlight our physical accessibility. We are located on the 3rd Floor of 185 Montague St in a modernized, professional office building featuring a wheelchair-accessible elevator entrance, an accessible lobby, and fully accessible restroom facilities.

How close is your physical office location to public transportation lines?

We are located right adjacent to Chipotle, directly steps away from the Borough Hall Station (servicing the 2, 3, 4, and 5 trains) and the Court Street Station (servicing the M and R lines). A CitiBike station sits directly outside our front door, and a paid valet parking lot is located across the street.
