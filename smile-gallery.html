Your Journey from Consultation to Reveal

01

Aesthetic Discovery Session

Arrive to an upbeat welcome from our team, and relax in our traditional, residential lobby with a hot coffee or tea from our Keurig beverage bar. Our doctors will then take the time to listen closely to your cosmetic goals and address any situational dental anxieties in a calm, supportive environment.

02

3D Digital Smile Mapping

We swap old-fashioned, goopy molding trays for our high-speed 3Shape Trios Scanner. This advanced digital wand captures an ultra-precise 3D model of your teeth on screen, allowing our doctors to plan your custom smile contours with absolute accuracy.

03

Collaborative Clinical Engineering

Our doctors work as an interdisciplinary triage, reviewing your dental layout using advanced accredited methodologies. This ensures your aesthetic teeth shapes operate in complete harmony with your underlying jaw joint mechanics and chewing muscles.

04

The Custom Transformation Reveal

Relax in our treatment suites equipped with massaging operatory chairs and comforting blankets while we gently execute your plan under local numbing or nitrous oxide. Walk out with a beautifully restored smile and enjoy our refreshing, lavender-scented hot towel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the before-and-after photographs in your smile gallery real patients treated by your doctors?

Yes, absolutely. Every single case, porcelain veneer restoration, and clear aligner transformation displayed in our smile gallery features the actual, high-quality clinical work performed directly by Dr. Bellafiore, Dr. Black, and Dr. Baer for our local patient family.

What is your office policy if I need to adjust my consultation appointment?

Because our comprehensive smile transformations and cosmetic consultations require detailed digital setups and substantial blocks of dedicated doctor time, we require a strict 48 hour cancellation and rescheduling notice. Failure to provide a 48 hour notice will result in a transparent $125.00 fee. You can smoothly coordinate changes via text, email, or a phone call.

Can you show me a preview of my straight smile transformation before I begin aligner treatment?

Yes, we can. By utilizing our advanced 3Shape Trios digital scanner, we generate a high-resolution 3D layout of your teeth on screen. The integrated software allows Dr. Black or Dr. Baer to simulate your exact sequential movements, giving you a visual preview of your final straight results before your custom clear trays are even fabricated.

Will custom porcelain veneers match the exact shade of my surrounding natural teeth?

Yes, perfectly. We treat cosmetic smile design as a precise blend of science and art. Our doctors individually hand-select porcelain translucencies, custom edge contours, and specific teeth shades that seamlessly echo your surrounding anatomy, ensuring your smile looks naturally radiant and never artificial.

How long can I expect the results of a comprehensive full-mouth reconstruction to endure?

When engineered by our CDBH doctors and maintained with excellent home care, high-tier porcelain crowns, veneers, and dental implant restorations routinely last 10 to 15 years or longer. Wearing a custom hard-acrylic nightguard from our office protects your beautiful investment from the damaging forces of unconscious nighttime clenching.
