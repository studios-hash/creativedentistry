Frequently Asked Questions

How exactly does Masseter Botox help relieve my TMJ and jaw pain?

When therapeutic Allergan Botox is injected directly into your masseter muscles, it temporarily blocks the intense, involuntary nerve signals that cause severe jaw clenching and grinding. It targets the painful muscle spasms directly while leaving your normal ability to talk, laugh, and chew entirely natural.

What is your office policy regarding late cancellations or appointment adjustments?

Because our neuromuscular evaluations and therapeutic Botox sessions require highly customized preparation and extensive doctor time, we require a strict 48 hour notice to cancel or reschedule to avoid a $125.00 fee. You can easily adjust your visit via text, email, or phone.

Why are soft or store-bought nightguards bad for TMJ disorders?

Store-bought "boil-and-bite" nightguards are made of soft, squishy plastic. When your brain senses a soft material between your teeth during sleep, it automatically triggers a chewing reflex, causing you to grind and clench harder, drastically worsening your jaw pain. Our laboratory-fit guards are hard acrylic to ensure muscles relax.

How long can I expect the pain relief from a Masseter Botox treatment to last?

Most patients experience a profound reduction in jaw tension, morning headaches, and neck tightness within 7 to 10 days following treatment, and the relief lasts three to four months. Over time, as the overworked masseter muscle safely downsizes, the window of pain relief can extend significantly longer.

Can I utilize your private In-House Membership Plan to save on TMJ therapies?

Yes, absolutely. While our membership plan is structured to fully cover your routine annual dental cleanings, exams, and diagnostic X-rays, enrollment immediately unlocks explicit, transparent discounts on advanced therapeutic treatments and custom nightguards across our services.
