Aesthetic Smile Transformations Built to Look Natural

Custom Porcelain Veneers

Meticulously shaped, ultra-thin porcelain laminates are bonded securely to the front of your teeth to instantly correct permanent gaps, deep stains, and slightly crowded enamel lines.

Philips Zoom!® In-Office Teeth Whitening

Achieve a dramatically brighter smile in just a single appointment using our genuine, light-activated Zoom! system, offering safer, more uniform aesthetic results than store-bought options.

Professional Take-Home Whitening Kits

Medical-grade bleaching gels paired with personalized, lab-fabricated application trays allow you to safely lift deep surface stains at your own pace from home.

Composite Resin Bonding

A fast, conservative option to repair minor cosmetic flaws using high-grade, tooth-colored composite materials artistically sculpted to correct chips or spaces in one visit.

Enameloplasty & Tooth Contouring

Gentle, micro-reshaping of your teeth enamel can smooth away jagged edges, correct slight overlaps, and balance your overall smile symmetry beautifully.

Full Mouth Smile Reconstructions

For highly complex aesthetic or worn-down smiles, our team coordinates a phased, comprehensive treatment timeline to fully restore your biting mechanics and structural beauty.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will custom porcelain veneers look obviously fake, bulky, or unnatural?

No, absolutely not. Our "North Star" is to deliver the highest-quality clinical work possible, which means focusing heavily on natural acoustics, customized color shading, and biological tooth proportions that match your facial symmetry.

Can professional composite bonding close a noticeable gap between my front teeth?

Yes, composite bonding is a fantastic, highly conservative, single-visit cosmetic solution. Dr. Black or Dr. Baer can artfully build up and sculpt the tooth-colored resin to close spaces cleanly with minimal to no alteration of your natural enamel.

Does your office charge an administration fee if I need to adjust my cosmetic appointment?

We strictly enforce a clear 48 hour cancellation and rescheduling window. Because our cosmetic and full-mouth appointments require substantial preparation and dedicated doctor time, late adjustments or failures incur a transparent $125.00 fee. You can easily adjust your visit via text, email, or phone.

How long can I expect my professional porcelain veneers to last?

When designed by a Pankey-trained professional and kept healthy with routine hygiene, modern porcelain veneers routinely last 10 to 15 years or longer. Utilizing a custom protective nightguard from our office safeguards your cosmetic porcelain investment from unconscious nighttime grinding.

Is the Philips Zoom! in-office whitening procedure safe for individuals with thin enamel?

Yes, our light-activated Philips Zoom! treatments are entirely medical-grade and executed under professional supervision. We perform an evaluation first and integrate specialized desensitizing protocols to ensure your whitening experience is completely comfortable and enamel-safe.
