Advanced Prosthodontics Crafted to Fit Your Life

Full Complete Dentures

Custom-tailored, tissue-shaded prosthetics engineered meticulously to replace an entire upper or lower arch of missing teeth, fully restoring your facial fullness and profile.

Removable Partial Dentures

Lightweight, highly durable frameworks designed to replace select missing teeth, blending seamlessly and locking securely alongside your remaining natural teeth.

Fixed Hybrid Dentures (Fixed Full Arch)

Non-removable, streamlined arches anchored permanently to strategically placed dental implants, offering maximum chewing force and zero palate coverage.

Implant-Supported Overdentures

Advanced removable denture options that snap securely onto titanium implant posts, completely eliminating shifting, speech clicking, and messy adhesives.

Precision Occlusal Balancing

Meticulous calibration of your denture's bite path to distribute forces evenly across your jaw joints, preventing chronic muscle fatigue and sore spots.

Who Are Custom Dentures Designed For?

  • Individuals experiencing extensive tooth loss or dealing with worn-out, slipping traditional dentures.
  • Patients looking to regain the freedom to eat comfortably, speak clearly, and laugh without fear of prosthetic movement.
  • Neighbors experiencing facial collapsing or deep wrinkles around the mouth caused by missing teeth and lost bone structure.
  • Insured or uninsured individuals looking for robust prosthodontic care backed by flexible payment terms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my custom complete dentures slip, slide, or cause clicking noises?

No. Traditional, poorly fitting dentures click because they lack stability. Dr. Baer utilizes advanced 3D scanning and bite trial blueprints to ensure your custom dentures contour tightly to your unique ridges, maximizing natural retention and clear speech.

What is your administrative policy if I need to reschedule a denture appointment?

We schedule extensive, dedicated blocks of time for our custom prosthodontic steps. We require a strict 48 hour cancellation notice to avoid a $125.00 fee. You can easily adjust your visit with our desk via text, email, or a phone call.

How do I properly care for my new removable partial denture overnight?

Always remove your partial denture before sleeping to allow your gum tissues to rest. Brush the appliance thoroughly using a soft-bristled denture brush and a non-abrasive cleaner, and store it safely in clean, room-temperature water overnight to maintain its precise shape.

Am I a candidate to transition from a loose removable denture to a fixed hybrid denture?

Most patients are excellent candidates. By combining an evaluation with our low-radiation 3D CBCT imaging, Dr. Bellafiore can analyze your jawbone density to see if implant-supported fixed dentures are the right fit to restore your permanent chewing power.

Reclaim Stable Speech, Total Comfort, and Secure Function

Rushed, poorly fitted traditional dentures frequently slip, irritate gum tissue, and make natural pronunciation difficult. Dr. Baer utilizes her hospital-honed Chief Residency prosthodontic training and advanced Pankey bite-mapping protocols to construct rock-solid partials, complete full dentures, or secure implant-supported overdentures engineered to distribute chewing forces perfectly.
