Streamlined Clear Aligner Orthodontics

Straighten your teeth discreetly without the cumbersome metal brackets, wires, or lifestyle restrictions of traditional braces. Our custom clear aligner therapies utilize advanced 3D scanning technology to map out a fast, nearly invisible path to your ideal smile.

Modern Orthodontic Correction Without the Metal ● [ H3 + BODY ] Straumann ClearCorrect Aligners — Utilizing medical-grade, highly translucent, stain-resistant clear aligner trays tailored precisely to shift your teeth into alignment comfortably. ● [ H3 + BODY ] Custom Post-Treatment Retainers — Tailored Hawley, Essix, or durable fixed retainers engineered specifically to lock your teeth into their final, beautiful positions and prevent natural shifting over time.

Who Are Clear Aligners For?

  • Busy working professionals and teens looking to correct teeth crowding or spacing gaps without the "metal mouth" look.
  • Adults experiencing orthodontic relapse who wore traditional metal braces in the past and want to gently realign their smiles.
  • Individuals looking for a removable orthodontic solution that allows them to eat whatever they want and maintain easy oral hygiene.
  • Insured or uninsured Brooklyn neighbors seeking an advanced, clear path to a straighter smile backed by low monthly financing.
