Systemic Digital Investments for Patient Safety & Comfort

We believe our standard of care is only as powerful as the diagnostic dental technology backing it. Our Brooklyn Heights practice replaces traditional clinical guesswork with advanced, non-invasive digital workflows that actively prioritize your physical comfort, minimize time spent in the chair, and deliver extreme diagnostic accuracy.

Less Friction. Safer Procedures. Faster Recovery.

Whether we are planning computer-guided missing tooth replacements, capturing putty-free digital records, or executing gentle gum therapies, our office leverages premium, name-brand medical machinery. By combining high-definition 3D CBCT Wellness Scans, the ultra-precise 3Shape Trios Digital Scanner, and the minimally invasive Biolase Waterlase Laser system, we ensure your treatments are highly predictable and entirely optimized for a pain-free healing experience.

Insurance, Financing, & Membership Portals

To ensure that premium dental care remains completely accessible and stress-free for your household budget, we offer structured financial options tailored to your specific insurance situation.

Commercial Insurance Support

We work directly with most major PPO dental insurance plans as an out-of-network provider, handling all complex claims and paperwork on your behalf to ensure you maximize your reimbursement benefits without the administrative hassle (See our dedicated Insurance page for more details). We also maintain active, direct network participation with Delta Dental and CSEA.

The In-House Dental Membership Alternative

For our local neighbors completely without traditional commercial insurance coverage, we provide an affordable private alternative. Enrolling in our In-House Membership Plan fully covers your recommended 2 routine checkups/cleanings per year, all necessary diagnostic digital X-rays, 1 emergency exam , and unlocks clear, transparent treatment discounts across our services.

Flexible Monthly Payment Financing

If you are undertaking comprehensive cosmetic updates, structural implants, or full-mouth transformations, our front desk team can instantly help you apply for flexible, low-interest monthly financing through Cherry and Sunbit. These options feature fast approvals and soft credit checks to keep your monthly budget completely comfortable (Review options on our Financing page).
